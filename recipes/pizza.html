Homemade Pizza

Description

Embark on a culinary adventure with our homemade pizza recipe, where every bite is a celebration of flavor and creativity. Begin by crafting the perfect pizza dough, mixing flour, water, yeast, and a pinch of salt until it forms a supple ball of dough. Allow it to rise until doubled in size, then roll it out into your desired shape, whether round, rectangular, or even rustic. Next, lavish on a vibrant tomato sauce, bursting with the tangy sweetness of ripe tomatoes and fragrant herbs. Layer on your favorite toppings, from classic pepperoni and mushrooms to gourmet combinations like prosciutto and arugula. Finish with a generous blanket of gooey mozzarella cheese, ensuring each slice is a melty masterpiece. Bake until golden and bubbling, then slice and savor the homemade goodness with friends and family. With endless possibilities for customization, this homemade pizza recipe promises to delight taste buds and bring joy to any gathering.

Ingredients

    Dough

  • 1 (0.25 oz.) envelope active dry yeast
  • 1 cup lukewarm water
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp shortening
  • 1/4 tsp salt

    • Sauce

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup chpped onion
  • 1 (6 oz.) can tomato paste
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp dried marjoram
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • 1/8 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/8 tsp ground black pepper

    • Cheese

  • 1 (12 oz.) shredded Mozzarella cheese

    • Equipment

  • 1 large bowl
  • 1 rolling pin
  • 1 saucepan
  • 2 pizza pans

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Make the dough: Dissolve yeast in lukewarm water in a small bowl. Let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  3. Combine flour, shortening, and salt in a large bowl; add yeast mixture and stir until dough comes together. Knead dough on a lightly floured surface until smooth and elastic, about 8 minutes.
  4. Lightly oil a large bowl. Place dough into the bowl and turn to coat with oil. Cover with a damp cloth and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 45 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, make the sauce: Heat oil in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and saut√© until tender, about 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in tomato paste, water, salt, sugar, oregano, garlic powder, basil, marjoram, cumin, chili powder, red pepper flakes, and black pepper; simmer for 15 to 20 minutes.
  7. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  8. Divide dough in half. Roll each half into a 12-inch circle. Place dough onto two 12-inch pizza pans; cover with sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until crusts are golden brown, about 20 minutes.
